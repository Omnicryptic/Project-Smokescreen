Here is the central point of all things Omnicryptic, the pseudonym under which I publish all of my not-so-various hobby projects. Currently, my interests are drifting around the area of simulations and games, and this interest manifests itself in the form of Project Smokescreen, a world simulation inspired by Dwarf Fortress , the brainchild of Tarn and Zach Adams, although nowhere near as vast in terms of scope and complexity; especially now, in the beginning. More information about this project can be found below.

Bugs are to be expected, since I can't anticipate all the possible differences that my programs will encounter between computer systems. At present time though, I have set up no method of bug reporting, which is pretty essential to any independent project. However, bugs can be brought to my attention using methods listed in the Follow Me section, but if my projects ever get a notable number of followers, upgrades will need to happen.
